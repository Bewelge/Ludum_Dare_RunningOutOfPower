Keep the Earth alive as long as possible!
Use Your mouse to rotate the Sun around the Earth (or Earth around the Sun). Every time You make a (clockwise) Rotation, one day passes and You will gain some Energy.
Don't go too close to the sun or the Earth will burn!
But don't let it get too far away or humanity will freeze!
Also don't let the earth get hit by the meteors! Use the sun instead to burn them all down!
Have Fun!
